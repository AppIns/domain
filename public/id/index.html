Alex id to connect with classmates





Sign up in seconds, continue using for years.

You can create an id that can be used to share to other people, easily share contact information, or just to have some easy way for other people to reach you. Once you create your id, you may do whatever you want with it. You can publish it online or share it with a very select group. Personally, I use it for when a classmate asks for a phone number or email address. It's much easier to give them a short link than a random number or long email address. You can also be sure they will have a way to reach you, either by email, phone, or even GitHub.

If you need your id deleted for any reason, contact me through email. If your email address matches the one on the id, the process will be very quick to take down. If your email address doesn't match, it will take longer to take down the id.
